Street Fighter V (ストリートファイターV Sutorīto Faitā Faivu?) is an upcoming fighting video game produced by Capcom, which co-developed the game together with Dimps. It is the fifth main numbered entry in Capcom's Street Fighter franchise. The game is set to be released in February 2016[2] for the PlayStation 4, Microsoft Windows, and Linux, and will feature cross-platform play between the three platforms.
